Financial & Compliance Solutions Powered by AI

We at Plutus empower companies with AI-based solutions for financial management, labour law compliance, and other essential business services. Our expertise lies in the integration of automation, data-driven solutions, and predictive analytics to enhance operations, guarantee adherence to regulations, and promote sustainable expansion.

Through the combination of AI-powered automation with deep industry insights, we enable companies to become more efficient, improve decision-making, and lower risks.

How Plutus Simplifies Business Operations through AI?

We leverage AI to:

  • 1

    Enhance Financial Management: Automate bookkeeping, financial reporting, and strategic planning.

  • 2

    Guarantee Compliance: Use AI to track labour laws, PF/ESI rules, and tax compliance.

  • 3

    Streamline Payroll & HR: Automate salary processing, payslip generation, and compliance with labour laws.

  • 4

    Optimize Risk & Governance: Identify risks in advance, ensure transparency, and improve internal controls.

  • 5

    Digitize Document Management: Securely store compliance documents on the cloud.

AI-Enabled Services

  • AI-Powered Financial Management

    We utilize AI to automate accounting, produce real-time financial reports, and maximize cash flow. With no manual errors and delays, companies can achieve correct financial insights, allowing for strategic decision-making.

  • AI-Powered Tax & Compliance Assistance

    With accurate and timely filings, our AI-based solutions continuously monitor tax liabilities. Automated monitoring of compliance prevents companies from incurring penalties and regulatory threats, keeping them in sync with tax regulations.

  • AI-Powered Payroll & HR Compliance

    AI makes payroll easy, with correct processing of salaries, generating payslips, and deduction of taxes. AI also makes labour law compliance easy by automating the management of employees' records and statutory returns.

  • AI-Enabled MIS & Reporting

    With dashboards powered by AI and automatic reports, companies receive current financial information in real-time. Companies can enhance budgeting, forecasting, and general decision-making with the key parameters analyzed through metrics.

  • AI-Facilitated Controls & Risk Management

    AI enhances governance through early detection of financial risks, fraud, and compliance violations. Automated internal controls ensure transparency and regulatory compliance in businesses.

  • AI-Enabled Compliance with Labour Laws

    AI enables firms to monitor and administer statutory dues such as PF, ESI, and other labour law statutes. Automated reminder and reporting result in timely compliance, minimizing legal exposures.

  • Digital & Cloud-Based Compliance Solutions

    Our cloud-based AI solutions allow companies to store and manage compliance documents securely. With enhanced search and retrieval capabilities, companies can access financial records, tax returns, and regulatory files in a timely manner, facilitating easy compliance and collaboration.
